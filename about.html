About Me

I’m Melody — a graphic designer & product designer from Toronto. I’ve been designing logos, print media and more for years, and am currently working full time as a UX/UI designer. I love the mix of creativity and powerful communication through great design, whether it’s a concise logo, a beautiful poster, an intuitive user experience, or anything else!


I have been working as a designer for years, and have extensive experience in most of the Adobe Suite (Especially Illustrator, Photoshop and InDesign) designing logos, print media, web media and more. I currently use Figma for UX/UI design and take regular contract work as a video editor with Adobe After Effects.
